About

I specialize in turning data into meaningful insights that support smart decisions, improve processes, and drive sustainable growth. With expertise in Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI, I’ve supported institutions like the Accra Metropolitan Assembly in building dashboards, conducting impact assessments, reporting, analyzing data for planning and enhancing monitoring and evaluation results.

Currently integrating safe and responsible AI tools and automation to optimize reporting processes and enhance data-driven strategy execution. Open to data-focused roles and collaborative projects that promote business growth, inclusive development, sustainability, humanitarian impact, and community resilience.

Skills, Tools & Software

Data Collection & Preparation

I specialize in end-to-end data workflows from collection to preparation for analysis. I ensure that the relevant data is collected from the most relevant sources. I utilize SQL, Excel, and Python to extract, validate and clean, and automate and process large datasets.

Analysis & Interpretation

I focus on uncovering patterns, measuring impact, and deriving actionable insights. I leverage Python libraries and Excel to perform descriptive and inferential statistical analyses, and use SQL to execute complex queries, joins, and aggregations across large-scale relational databases. My emphasis is on meaningful findings that align with project goals, key performance indicators, and strategic questions.

Communication & presentation

I communicate insights to diverse stakeholders through data-driven reports, dynamic dashboards, and visual analytics tailored to both technical and non-technical audiences. I use Power BI, Excel, and Python visualization libraries to ensure clarity, relevance, and strategic alignment enabling informed decision making and measurable impact across the organization.
